Walks & park visits 🌳

Daily updates

Photos & videos, every day

You'll get photos and videos every single day - the thing owners mention most. The walks, the naps, the new friends. You never have to wonder how they're getting on.

A real home, never a kennel

Your dog stays in our cozy home in Fulham, never a row of cages. We only ever host two or three at a time, so the day stays calm, not chaotic.

The same faces, every time

The same small team every visit: Ivo, Natalie and Luna. No rotating staff, no strangers. The people who meet your dog on day one are the ones who look after them throughout.

A free meet & greet first

Come round for a free meet & greet before you book a thing. No commitment, just a chance for you and your dog to see the home, meet us, and decide it feels right.
