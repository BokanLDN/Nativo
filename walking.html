The walk itself

Pickup, an hour out, a clean dog home.

Every walk starts with pickup at your home and ends with a clean dog back where they belong. In between, it's an hour at your local park: Eel Brook, Bishop's Park, South Park or Brompton Cemetery, depending on what suits your dog best.

Energetic dogs get the longer routes. Older or more nervous dogs get the quieter ones. Everyone gets fresh water, a towel down if it's wet and, of course, free treats for good behaviour.

After every walk

You'll get a short message from us: photos, the route we took, who your dog played with, and any notes worth flagging. It's how I learn what your dog likes so the next walk's even better.
